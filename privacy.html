Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Our Commitment

LottoGen is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information. Our service is designed to be simple and transparent, and our approach to privacy reflects that.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be crystal clear: we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. You can use our Lotto Number Generator without providing any personal data.

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not store the numbers you generate.
  • We do not use tracking cookies that identify you personally.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

To improve our website and ensure a good user experience, we may collect anonymous, non-personal information automatically. This includes:

  • Log Data: Like most websites, our servers may log anonymous data such as your browser type, device type, and the pages you visit. This data is used for statistical analysis and cannot be used to identify you.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-friendly analytics tools to understand how users interact with our site (e.g., which features are most popular). This data is aggregated and does not contain personal information.

3. Third-Party Services (Google AdSense)

To support our website, we may display advertisements from Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. For more information on how Google collects and uses data, please see the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
